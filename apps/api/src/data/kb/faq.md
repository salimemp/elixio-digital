# Elixio Digital — Frequently Asked Questions

## What is Elixio Digital?

Elixio Digital is a marketplace where creators sell digital products
(templates, presets, 3D models, brushes, courses, code, design assets)
and buyers discover and download them. We focus on fair pricing (no
hidden platform tax), global reach (127 tax regions), and accessibility
(WCAG 2.1 AA, 42 languages, voice control).

## Is Elixio free to use?

Yes — creating an account is free. Buyers can browse and purchase
without a subscription. Creators pay a small transaction fee on each
sale (no monthly fee).

## How do I create an account?

Go to /auth/register and pick "I want to buy" or "I want to sell".
You'll need an email address. We support email + password, magic link
login, Google, GitHub, and passkeys (Touch ID / Face ID / hardware
keys).

## Can I use Elixio in my language?

Yes — the interface is translated into 42 languages including English,
Spanish, French, German, Hindi, Portuguese, Arabic, Chinese (Simplified
and Traditional), Japanese, Korean, and 30 more. Switch language from
the globe icon in the top-right of any page.

## Is my data safe?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption in transit (HTTPS) and at
rest. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. We support two-factor
authentication (TOTP) and passkeys. We're GDPR, CCPA, and PIPEDA
compliant. You can export or delete your account at any time from
/profile/privacy or /profile/delete.

## How do I get help?

You can ask the Elixio assistant (the chat bubble at the bottom-left
of every page), email support@elixiodigital.com, or read the docs at
/docs.
