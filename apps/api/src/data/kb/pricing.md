# Pricing and fees

## Buyer prices
All prices on Elixio are shown in your local currency when supported
(127 regions). Tax is calculated and shown at checkout. There are no
hidden fees on the buyer side.

## Creator fees
Creators keep 90% of the sale price. Elixio takes 10% to cover:
- Payment processing (Stripe / Razorpay)
- Cloud storage (Cloudflare R2)
- CDN delivery
- Customer support
- Platform development

There are no monthly fees, no listing fees, and no fees for free
assets. You only pay when you make a sale.

## Payouts
- Minimum payout: $10 USD equivalent
- Schedule: weekly, every Tuesday
- Method: Stripe Connect (135+ countries) or Razorpay (India)
- Currency: payouts are made in your connected account's default
  currency. We don't auto-convert.

## Refunds
If a buyer requests a refund within 7 days and the asset is
defective or not as described, we issue a full refund. The creator's
payout for that sale is reversed.
