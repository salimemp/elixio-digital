The True Cost to Sell Digital Products: Marketplace Fees Explained

Marketplace fees come in several flavors, and understanding each one is critical to maximizing your earnings. You have transaction fees, listing fees, monthly subscription costs, and sometimes payout fees that all stack on top of each other.

When you sell digital products, the platform fee is just one piece of the puzzle. Payment processing fees from Stripe, PayPal, or other gateways also apply, and these typically run around 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction on most platforms.

The real issue arises when platforms add a marketplace commission on top of processing fees. Creative Market, for example, charges a 50% commission, meaning a $20 sale puts only $10 in your pocket before processing fees are even deducted.

This is why a thorough platform fees comparison is essential before committing to any single platform. Even a 5% difference in fees can mean thousands of dollars over a year of steady sales.
