How to Choose the Right Platform for Your Digital Assets

Choosing where to sell digital products is not a one-size-fits-all decision. The right platform depends on what you sell, your price points, and how much traffic you can drive yourself.

Consider Your Product Type

Different platforms excel with different types of digital assets. If you sell procreate brushes or fonts, a design-focused marketplace like Creative Market might drive more organic sales. If you sell notion templates, a platform with strong subscription and update features matters more. Elixio and Lemon Squeezy both handle product updates well. If you sell code online, you need licensing support and secure delivery. Elixio's end-to-end encrypted delivery and Elixio's digital library make it a strong choice for developers.

Calculate Your True Cost per Sale

Do not just look at the headline platform fee. Factor in payment processing, per-transaction flat fees, and any monthly subscription costs. For example, a platform charging 5% + 50 cents per transaction may cost more than a 10% flat fee platform if your average sale price is under $5. Always run the numbers for your specific product mix.

Evaluate Marketplace Discovery

Some platforms, like Elixio and Creative Market, have built-in marketplaces where buyers can discover your products. Others, like Payhip and Ko-fi, require you to drive all your own traffic. A built-in creator marketplace can be worth a slightly higher fee if it brings you customers you would not have reached otherwise. Consider how much you currently spend on marketing.

Check Payout Speed and Flexibility

How quickly do you get paid? Gumroad offers weekly payouts, while other platforms may hold funds for longer periods. Elixio emphasizes fast, flexible payouts as part of its creator-first philosophy. If cash flow is important to your business, payout speed should be a key factor in your decision. Read the fine print on each platform's payout policies.

Think About Scalability

Your platform should grow with you. A 5% fee difference might not matter at $500 per month, but at $50,000 per month, it is $2,500 every month. Choose a platform that will not punish your success with escalating fees. Elixio's flat 5% model scales cleanly, while platforms with tiered pricing may surprise you as you grow.
